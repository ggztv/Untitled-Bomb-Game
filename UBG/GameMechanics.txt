Game name: Untitled Bomb Game
Shortened name: UBG

UBG is a Roblox game about hiding and finding bombs

Info: Info.text
Paths to UI, Bomb, Defuser (Fuse Cutter): Paths.txt

Server:
Max. Players: 12
Style: Retro Roblox

UI:
In lobby:
Screen top: Settings (Music volume, SFX volume, Name editor, Shadow toggle)
Screen Bottom: Play, Tasks (Taskmanager), Shop (Work in progress, for future updates), Credit counter.

In game:
Screen top: Settings, Score (Red team, blue team), timer.
Screen bottom: Inventory, Bombs left counter.

Gameplayer loop:
1. Player 1, Player 2, Press Play
2. Teleport into game map
3. Start hiding bombs for 90 seconds or until all bombs are planted
4. Swap maps, start finding and defusing bombs. (90 Seconds)
5. First to 5 (We need to change it to first to 3), Intermission lasts 5 seconds.

Win condition:
Player who defuses all bombs first
Or: Player who defused more bombs by timer end

Lose condition:
Player who didn't defuse bombs before other player didn
Or: Less bombs defused than other player by timer end

General info:
Currency name: Credits

Currency value: Very high (Very low amount of credits are able to be gained per day)
